## Overview

As Software Coordinator at **Tensor, IOE Thapathali**, I co-organized a C programming workshop series aimed at first-year engineering students who were just beginning their programming journey.

## Goal

Bridge the gap between theoretical coursework and practical coding skills for incoming students, giving them a solid foundation in C programming and problem-solving.

## What We Did

- Designed a structured curriculum covering C fundamentals, data structures, and problem-solving patterns
- Delivered hands-on sessions with live coding demonstrations and exercises
- Created a collaborative environment where students could ask questions and learn from each other
- Coordinated with teammates to ensure consistent quality across sessions

## Impact

The workshop helped dozens of first-year students build confidence in programming early in their academic journey. Many participants went on to contribute to Tensor's projects and community events.

## Role

As Software Coordinator, I was responsible for curriculum planning, session facilitation, and fostering an inclusive, collaborative learning environment within the Tensor community.
