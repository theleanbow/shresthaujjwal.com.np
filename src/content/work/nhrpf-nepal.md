## Overview

I contributed to the **National Human Rights Protection Forum for the Disabled, Nepal (NHRPF)** in two distinct roles over two years.

## Web Developer (Sep 2022 – Oct 2022)

- Designed and built the organization website using WordPress CMS
- Implemented user management with different permission levels for staff and volunteers
- Set up and configured email accounts in Microsoft Outlook for efficient organizational communication

## Project Manager / Volunteer (Nov 2022 – Dec 2023)

- Drafted and submitted 20+ grant proposals to support the organization's programs
- Organized both physical and digital office files and documents for smooth operations
- Supervised volunteers involved in producing audio books for people with disabilities
- Organized audio book distribution programs to reach beneficiaries across Nepal
- Committed to making a positive impact in the non-profit and disability rights sector

## Skills Applied
